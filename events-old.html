Here are the top reasons for choosing to study in the UAE:
  • The UAE is the top destination for students in the Gulf region
  • With cities such as Dubai and Abu Dhabi, the UAE is the top destination forinternational students
  • The higher educational system is modelled on western educational systems withpartnership universities in the UK, Australia, USA, etc
  • Three state-funded universities and more than 50 private institutions
  • Courses are taught in English at most universities, so there is no need to learn Arabic
  • International students are allowed to work part-time while holding a student visa
  • With a booming economy and tax-free salaries, there are many exciting jobopportunities for new graduates
  • Tuition fees and the cost of living are relatively cheaper than in many westerncountries
  • A new golden visa scheme for talented students and their families.
