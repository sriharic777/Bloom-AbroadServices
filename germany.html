Here are the top reasons for choosing to study in the UAE:
  • The UAE is the top destination for students in the Gulf region
  • With cities such as Dubai and Abu Dhabi, the UAE is the top destination forinternational students
  • The higher educational system is modelled on western educational systems withpartnership universities in the UK, Australia, USA, etc
  • Three state-funded universities and more than 50 private institutions
  • Courses are taught in English at most universities, so there is no need to learn Arabic
  • International students are allowed to work part-time while holding a student visa
  • With a booming economy and tax-free salaries, there are many exciting jobopportunities for new graduates
  • Tuition fees and the cost of living are relatively cheaper than in many westerncountries
  • A new golden visa scheme for talented students and their families.

Application Process

  • Step 1
    Admissions
    • Admissions to universities in the UAE take place in two sessions, either the

      winter semester, i.e. Dec-Jan, or the spring semester, i.e. Feb-Mar.

  • Step 2
    Applications
    • All graduate applicants are required to pay a non-refundable application fee rangingfrom AED 200 – 600.
  • Step 3
    Exams and Tests
    • Proof of English language skills - Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL):TOEFL ITP (score of 550), or TOEFL IBT (score of 79), or International EnglishLanguage Testing System (IELTS Academic version) (score of 6), if available at thetime of submitting the application. Only one of the tests is required.
  • Step 4
    Education Certificates
    • Original equivalency letter from the UAE Ministry of Education for certificates issued from outside the UAE.
    • This can be obtained after the applicant’s arrival in the UAE as they will be given a period of one registered semester in which to submit the letter.
    • Proof of 12 years of schooling and a bachelor’s degree needs to be attested by the Ministry of Higher Education.
  • Step 5
    Accommodations
    • If accommodation is required, the housing form and the medical form will need to befilled in and forwarded. They will be attached to the admission offer.
